PROBLEM

When people often go to prison they serve their sentence, pay their dues and get back outside. But the time they spend outside isn't very long at all. Many of them, 15%, get right back into prison for the same crime they comitted. This is a problem because our prisons are already overcrowded. 188,262 people although our prison capacity is of 170,772. This means that 18,000 people don't have a bed and all of them have to share limited living space with a lot more people than they should.

The money it takes from all taxpayers is quite significant to care for all prisioners. We need to find solutions so that people don't get back to prison and find a better living quality for the people that are there already.

-

SOLUTIONS

One of the solutions was already found in California. They teach inmates how to code and creat a buisness plan for when they get out. So when they reach the end of their time they have the knowledge on how to survive without relying on their previous criminal tendencies.

This helps inmates and taxpayers. If they don't come back then they don't add to the already enourmus prison population

Other solutions may be implemented. We can send even fewer people into prisons for drug possesion charges. Since they are very common and in most cases this people are a non threat to the society.

Allow drug offenders to serve less time in jails.
